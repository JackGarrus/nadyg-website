## Quick intro

To people that are coming from the Web Front-End world that don't have a Computer Science degree
having to do with system configurations in order to start developing our beloved CRUD apps feels like
a tedious task, specially for the beginners. I remember I hated this part to the core, until I got
tired of not understanding why the hell my machine wasn't working properly all of a sudden just because
I added a new tool that was updating my environmental variable, my $PATH.
